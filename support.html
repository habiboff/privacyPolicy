Frequently Asked Questions

How does By Smart Shield analyze products?

By Smart Shield uses advanced AI technology to analyze cosmetic product ingredients from photos.

What should I do if the app isn't working?

Please ensure you have the latest version installed and try restarting the app. If the issue persists, please contact our support team.

How can I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium directly within the app through the subscription page to access unlimited scans and advanced features.

Is my data secure?

Yes, By Smart Shield takes your privacy seriously. We do not store your login information or track your activities. Additionally, images are not stored and are always deleted. Data is only used for analysis purposes and we never share it with third parties for advertising or other purposes.
