SmartShield Privacy Policy

Introduction

At By Smart Shield, we respect and are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains the information that is collected, used, and protected while using our application.

Information Collected

Our application requires access to the following:

  • Camera and Photo Gallery Access: Access is provided to take photos or select from the gallery with user permission for cosmetic product analysis.
  • Apple ID Information: When signing in with Apple, only the minimum required information is collected for authentication purposes.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases for premium features are managed through Apple's StoreKit system.

Use of Information

The collected information is not used for any purpose.

Data Security

The security of user data is important to us. We take the following precautions:

  • All data is stored in encrypted form
  • Secure SSL connections are used
  • Regular security updates are performed

Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. If a parent or guardian learns that their child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify our users when significant changes are made.
