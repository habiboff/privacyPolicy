Balans Minus Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Balans Minus, we respect and are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains the information that is collected, used, and protected while using our application.

Information Collected

Our application requires access to the following:

  • Camera Access: Access is provided to scan QR codes on VAT refund receipts with user permission for expense tracking.
  • Internet Connection: Required to retrieve receipt information from external sources after QR code scanning.

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing QR codes from VAT refund receipts to automatically add expense information
  • Generating financial statistics and charts based on your expenses

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal financial information to any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All your financial data, including expenses and statistics, is stored locally on your device using Swift Data. We do not store your financial information on external servers. Your data remains private and under your control at all times.

Data Security

The security of user data is important to us. We take the following precautions:

  • We do not collect or store your receipt images

Third-Party Services

Our application uses external services only to retrieve receipt information after QR code scanning. These services are used exclusively for obtaining receipt details and do not have access to your personal financial data or statistics.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete all your financial data at any time through the app settings. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify our users when significant changes are made through the application.
